Who Am I?

I’m an Electrical & Computer Engineering student with a strong interest in designing systems that bridge hardware, software and intelligent algorithms. My work spans embedded devices, control systems and applied machine learning - often with a focus on making technology more accessible and practical in everyday use. I enjoy the challenge of turning ideas into working prototypes. What excites me most is creating solutions that are both technically sound and elegantly designed. Beyond academics, I value collaboration, curiosity, and continuous learning. Qualities that will help me grow as both an engineer and a problem-solver.

My industry experience from vacation work has given me practical exposure to engineering challenges, while my studies have strengthened my skills in Networks, Control Systems, Programming and many other electrical engineering sects. I enjoy communicating and sharing knowledge, which I developed further by tutoring various courses. I see myself as adaptable, collaborative and committed to delivering thoughtful solutions and I am eager to bring this balance of technical ability and interpersonal strengths to any future roles.
